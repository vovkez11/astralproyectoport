Aftercare

To keep your new tattoo looking sharp and healing properly, follow these steps.

Keep it Covered

Leave the bandage on for at least 2-4 hours after getting your tattoo.

Wash Gently

Use lukewarm water and fragrance-free soap to clean your tattoo daily.

Moisturize

Apply a thin layer of aftercare ointment to keep your tattoo hydrated.

Avoid Soaking

No swimming, hot tubs, or long baths while your tattoo is healing.

Stay Out of the Sun

Keep your tattoo covered or use SPF 30+ sunscreen to prevent fading.

No Picking

Let scabs and peeling skin fall off naturally to avoid scarring.
